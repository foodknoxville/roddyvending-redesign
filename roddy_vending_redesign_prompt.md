# RODDY VENDING WEBSITE REDESIGN — COWORK PROJECT PROMPT

## PROJECT OVERVIEW

You are building a complete single-page website redesign for Roddy Vending Company, Inc. — a local vending, micro-market, coffee service, food service, and catering company based in Knoxville, TN (est. 1965, vending industry since 1989). The current site (roddyvending.com) is a 2016 VendCentral template with stock photos and generic copy. This redesign needs to look modern, professional, and competitive with national players like Five Star Breaktime Solutions (fivestarbreaktime.com) — but built in a single day using AI tools.

This project is a demonstration piece. The person commissioning it (Donnie McClanahan) is showing the company owner (Jim) what AI can do for the business. The site itself needs to be legitimately good — not a gimmick, not a demo. Something Jim could point a prospect at tomorrow and feel proud of.

## DESIGN DIRECTION

**Aesthetic:** Clean, modern, professional. Not flashy or startup-trendy. This is a service company that has been operating since 1965. The design should communicate reliability, professionalism, and quiet confidence. Think: established company that finally got a website worthy of the business.

**Tone of copy:** Warm but professional. Local pride without being corny. Emphasize the human element — locally owned, real people, decades of relationships. Differentiate from the corporate feel of national competitors by leaning into the local/family-owned angle as a strength, not a limitation.

**Color palette suggestion:** Deep navy or charcoal as primary, a warm accent color (amber, copper, or a rich red), clean white space. Open to alternatives but avoid anything that looks like a generic SaaS landing page.

**Typography:** Clean sans-serif for body (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans, or similar), something with a bit more character for headings. No script fonts. No novelty fonts.

## COMPETITIVE CONTEXT

The primary local competitor is Five Star Breaktime Solutions (fivestarbreaktime.com). They are a PE-backed national company operating as a Canteen/Compass Group franchise.
