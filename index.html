Our Story

Six decades of showing up

Roddy Vending Company was founded in 1965 and has been operating in the vending industry since 1989. That is not a tagline. It is a track record. Sixty years of continuous operation means we have seen every trend, survived every economic cycle, and built the kind of relationships that only come from decades of consistent, reliable service.

We are not a franchise and we are not a subsidiary of a national chain. We are a locally owned company that runs its own commissary, employs its own people, and makes its own decisions. When something needs to change, we change it. When a customer has a problem, the person who picks up the phone has the authority to fix it.

That is what 60 years in business looks like. Not a corporate org chart. A handshake and a track record.
